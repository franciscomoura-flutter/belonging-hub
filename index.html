If you want to start learning about DEI... This journey consists of several paths designed to help you navigate the complexities of diversity, equity and inclusion - with an experience tailored to your reality.
You belong - and so does everyone else. Learn how to recognize bias, become a stronger ally, and contribute to a culture where everyone feels respected and included—no matter your role.
Lead with inclusion. As a manager, your impact is powerful. This path equips you with the tools to foster psychological safety, embrace difference, and support every team member in reaching their full potential.
Lead with inclusive hiring. As a hiring manager, the decisions you make shape not just your team, but the culture of your organization. This path equips you with the tools to recognize and mitigate bias, value diverse perspectives, and create a fair, welcoming hiring experience where every candidate has the opportunity to thrive.
